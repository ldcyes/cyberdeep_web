Guidelines

The Obsidian logo is inspired by its namesake, the volcanic rock which has been used since the dawn of humanity to make arrowheads, scrapers, knives, and other tools. Learn more.

Please do not edit, change, distort, recolor, or reconfigure the Obsidian logo.

The Obsidian name, logo and app icon are trademarks. You are free to customize the Obsidian app icon for your personal use. If you want to use Obsidian assets for commercial purposes, please contact us.
