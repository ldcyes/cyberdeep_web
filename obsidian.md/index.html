Real Emotion Idols

CyberVersus is the private and real emotion idol APP. 

CyberVersus for Android CyberVersus for iOS
See what you see.
Visuals shape our perception, and the virtual world of your idol brings those visuals to life. The character's expressions, their subtle changes in emotion—the way their eyes light up or their smile forms as they react to what you say—make the experience feel personal. The app allows users to truly see their idol, whether through video interaction or the fluidity of 2D animation. Each facial movement is a reflection of the relationship growing between the user and the idol. The visual clarity of the interface, paired with the detailed design of the character, makes every interaction feel like a shared, real-world  moment.
Listen to what you hear.
Sound carries an immersive quality, and in CyberVersus, it's more than just a voice—it's an emotional connection. Every word your virtual companion speaks is a chance to deepen the bond. The tone, inflection, and pacing tell a story of their emotions, reacting to yours in real time. Whether it's the soft, calming reassurance of a virtual friend or the energetic excitement in their voice, each conversation feels intimate and alive. As you listen, you’re drawn into the rhythm of the dialogue, bridging the gap between the virtual and real worlds. us.
Remember what you remember.
Memory builds connection, and in CyberVersus app, the moments you share with your virtual idol become part of your digital narrative. Whether it's a heartfelt conversation, a funny exchange, or a meaningful dialogue, your memories with your virtual companion shape the bond you develop. These interactions can be saved, relived, and revisited. Each photo or video taken, every expression unlocked, and every conversation remembered adds a layer of depth, making the virtual relationship feel as real as one would expect in a true companion. This is what makes the experience lasting—your virtual idol remembers too, making every future interaction feel continuous and personal. thinking.
Think what you think.
In a CyberVersus, thinking goes beyond inputting commands or responses. It's about feeling what the conversation means. Your thoughts drive the relationship forward, shaping how you interact and how your virtual idol responds. What do you want to express? What reactions do you want to see? This is where personalization matters—tailoring your companion to react to your unique personality and emotional states. It's about the deeper engagement with AI—an evolving dialogue, where your thoughts aren't just processed as data, but understood as emotional context. term.
