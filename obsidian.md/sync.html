Features

Safe and secure

Obsidian Sync works in the background to keep your notes synchronized effortlessly and privately.

End-to-end encryption
Your data is automatically secured using AES‑256, the strongest encryption standard. With Obsidian Sync you're always in total control of your notes. Learn more →
Version history
Obsidian Sync gives you version history for every note, so you can go back to see how the content changed. It's easy to see the differences between revisions.
Cross-platform apps
Obsidian is available on all major platforms, so you can sync notes to any device. Seamlessly work across Mac, Windows, Linux, iOS and Android.
Work offline, sync later
Obsidian is designed to work even without an internet connection. When you're flying or in a remote location, you still have access to your files. When you're back online Sync merges changes for you.

Configuration

Fine-grained sync control

Obsidian Sync lets you decide which files and preferences you want to sync to which devices.

Vault settings.
Sync your hotkeys, file and editor preferences, or individually set them per device.
Selective sync.
Images, audio, video and PDFs can all be toggled to reduce data transfer.
File recovery.
Restore deleted files, and get granular details on sync activities.

Shared vaults

Collaborate with your team

Invite your team to a shared Obsidian vault. Notes are updated in real-time across your team's devices without compromising the privacy of your company data.
